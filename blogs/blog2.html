Six skills to grow as a student for professional life

In this blog I am listing about six very important skills to grow as a student for his professional life.

1. Communication skills:

There is always been a emphasis given to students on developing communication skills. But this is one of the most under rated thing by the students. Communication skills are very much important in the vibrant corporate world to grow and survive. So students should work on improving ones communication skills while in college.

2. Problem solving and critical thinking :

Problem solving skills is one of the most important skill for engineers. The problem solving skill is the one that we acquire by more and more practice. Critical thinking is another skill associated to problem solving which Is very much important as well. Some problems need a very good critical thinking skill to solve them. Critical thinking is also one of the most checked ability of a candidates in face to face interviews.

3. Leadership skills:

Leaders are not born over night. Leadership is a skills which needs to be developed by an individual over a period of time. To be an excellent leader one has to know time management, problem solving ,situation tackling and decision making skills. Leadership skill not only refer to positional leadership but also situational leadership skills.

4. Time management and timing skills:

: time management which is one of the most stressed thing by elders to the new generation kids. In the modern world it is not only important to have time management skills but also timing is very important . Timing refers to when to execute a task and when not to. And to do right things at right time.

5. Decision making skills:

Great leaders are born by making good decision at perfect time. Decision making is one of the most important skill to have as an engineer. Rather than staying in dilemma it is always a good decision that makes one a successful man.

6. Teamwork and collaboration:

Team work and collaboration skills is one of the most important one to succeed in professional life. A good team player is always a boon to the company. When one works in a team it is always important to collaborate with the team and tasks ahead. So it is very important for an individual to develop team playing and collaboration skill.
